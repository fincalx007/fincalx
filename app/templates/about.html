About FinCalX

FinCalX is a finance education platform built around simple, private calculators for everyday money planning in India.

No login required

Educational purpose only

What FinCalX does

FinCalX helps users estimate SIP maturity values, loan EMIs, in-hand salary, and portfolio overlap without creating an account. The tools are designed for quick learning, comparison, and first-pass planning before you review official documents or consult a qualified professional.

Each calculator page includes context, formulas, examples, FAQs, and related tools so users can understand the assumptions behind the output. The goal is to make finance topics easier to explore, not to replace expert guidance.

The site does not provide personalized financial, investment, tax, legal, or lending advice. Results depend on your inputs and simplified assumptions, so they should be used as educational estimates only.
