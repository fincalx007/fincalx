{% endblock %} {% block explanation %} A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount every month in mutual funds. Over time, your investment benefits from compounding and rupee cost averaging, helping reduce risk and grow wealth steadily. {% endblock %} {% block formula %}

Future Value (FV) of SIP is calculated using:

FV = P × [((1 + r)n − 1) / r] × (1 + r)

Where:
P = Monthly investment
r = Monthly interest rate (annual rate / 12 / 100)
n = Total number of months
