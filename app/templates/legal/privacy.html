Legal

Privacy Policy

FinCalX is designed to be privacy-friendly, stateless, and simple to use without an account.

No Login or Account Data

This website does not provide login, signup, profile, or account features. Calculator inputs are processed only to generate the requested result and are not stored in an application database.

Calculator Inputs

SIP, EMI, income tax, and portfolio overlap inputs are used only for the calculation you submit. Do not enter sensitive personal identifiers, account numbers, PAN, Aadhaar, passwords, or confidential financial records.

Server Logs

Hosting infrastructure may create standard operational logs such as IP address, browser user agent, requested path, timestamp, and error information. These logs may be used for security, abuse prevention, uptime monitoring, and debugging.

Advertising and AdSense

Pages include advertising placements for future monetization. If Google AdSense or similar advertising is enabled, ad providers may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies and applicable consent requirements.

No Financial Data APIs

The website does not call external financial data APIs for the current calculators. Portfolio overlap is based on plain text entered by the user and does not verify market prices, quantities, or holdings.
